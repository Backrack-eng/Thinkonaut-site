Thinkonaut Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026

Thinkonaut is designed to help you capture and organize your thoughts. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains how your data is handled.

Information We Collect

Audio Recordings
When you choose to record, Thinkonaut captures audio using your device’s microphone.

Transcripts
Your recordings may be converted into text using speech recognition.

Generated Content
Thinkonaut may generate summaries, notes, lists, and structured insights from your recordings or written input.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used to:

  • Convert speech into text
  • Generate summaries and structured notes
  • Organize your ideas into usable formats
  • Allow you to view, edit, and export your content

Data Storage

Your recordings, transcripts, and notes are stored locally on your device.

Thinkonaut does not sell your data and does not use your data for advertising.

Third-Party Processing

To generate summaries, insights, or structured outputs, Thinkonaut may securely send portions of your content (such as transcripts or text input) to third-party services, including AI providers such as OpenAI.

These services process the data solely to generate results requested by you. Your data is not used for advertising.

Data Sharing

Your data is not shared with third parties except:

  • When necessary to provide transcription or AI-generated features
  • When you explicitly export or share your content

Permissions

Thinkonaut may request access to:

  • Microphone — to record your voice
  • Speech Recognition — to transcribe recordings
  • Notifications (optional) — to notify you when processing is complete

You can manage permissions at any time in your device Settings.

Your Control

You can:

  • Delete your notes and recordings at any time
  • Export your data from the app
  • Clear all stored data from within the app
