Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Fixamajig is a simple electronics troubleshooting app designed to help you fix common problems with your devices. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information the app uses and how.

Camera and Photos

Fixamajig uses your device’s camera and photo library solely to take or select a photo of the electronic device you are trying to fix. Photos are sent to Anthropic’s Claude AI service to identify your device and generate troubleshooting steps. Photos are not stored on our servers, not shared with third parties beyond Anthropic’s API, and not used for advertising or tracking.

Information You Type

Any text you type to describe your device, symptoms, or problem is sent to Anthropic’s Claude AI service to generate troubleshooting steps. Text is not stored on our servers, not shared with third parties beyond Anthropic’s API, and not used for advertising or tracking.

What We Don’t Collect

Fixamajig does not require an account. We do not collect your name, email, or location. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. We do not sell your data.

Permissions

Fixamajig may request access to your camera and photo library so you can capture or choose a photo of your device. You can manage or revoke these permissions at any time in your device Settings.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at hello@thinkonaut.app.
